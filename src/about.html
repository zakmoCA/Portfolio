About Me

Hello! I'm Zak, a 26-year-old aspiring full-stack developer with a background in respiratory science. My journey into programming began recently as a curiosity, and it is quickly evolving into a passion that has driven me to pursue a career as a developer. To develop my skills, I enrolled in Coder Academy, where I have been learning the ropes to land work as a full-stack developer.

Outside of programming, I enjoy running, reading, writing, and creating videos. These diverse interests not only keep me engaged but also inspire my creativity, which I apply to my work as a developer. As I continue to grow professionally, I am eager to delve deeper into backend development to enhance my understanding of the full spectrum of web development.
